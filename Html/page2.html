Every photograph shown on this page were taken during the spring semester of the 2019-2020 school year. These were used as the contents of my final project in my film black and white photography course. Since I had to turn my film camera back in to the school, I used my Canon Rebel t6 camera to take the photos shown on this page. I simply had to use a filter to convert all color photos taken from my camera to black and white photographs.
